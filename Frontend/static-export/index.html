How We Help

Our comprehensive approach addresses the root causes of period poverty through education, support, and empowerment.

Education

Comprehensive menstrual health education to break stigma and build confidence.

Support

Providing menstrual hygiene products and emotional support to girls in need.

Empowerment

Leadership training and life skills to help girls reach their full potential.
